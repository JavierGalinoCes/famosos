ALbert Boadella

Date of birth: 10 July 1943

He studied dramatic art in the Instituto de Teatro in Barcelona, in the Centre Dramatique de l'Est in Estrasburgo and corporal expression in París. When we was still studying he worked in the mime company of Italo Riccardi

In 1962, whenhe was 19, he founded with his mates Carlota Soldevilla and ANton Font, Els Joglars, the company that makes his whole career as actor, director and playwright. With Els Joglars he created more than 30 montages, each one being recognised and catalogued as good. His creations turn to have a critic and satiric charge, specially with the stablished power and wiht any factic power, making emphasis in the catholic Church.

His first big problem with authorities was in 2 of december in 1977. With La Torna a satire of the process Hinz Ches, he was jailed to be processed for making a was war tip for making fun of the spanish army. But one day before the view the protagonized an spectacular fugue out of jail and took refuge in France.

Being a bullfighting big fan and a public defensor of the art Cúchares, Boadella put this art over any other cause the emotion of the bullfighting ritual.-It doesnt exist such art with the power of conmoving the human more than this.[...] Through my life i have enjoyed the best expressiones of art in music, dance, opera and theatre, but nothing is compared to the bullfighting ritual- In december of 2006 he premiered in Madrid like the medieval discussions, tituled COntroversia del toro y el torero where you can see some reasons for and against this art.

Pep Guardiola

Date of birth: 18 January 1971

He was a famous footballer in the 90s and the fist years of the 2000s, after that he became a trainer and is still working on it nowadays. As a player, he was a midfield player that developed most of his career playing in the FC Barcelona during the 90s and the beginning of the 2000s, and he won 16 titles between nationals and internationals: six leagues, two King Cups, four supercups, an european supercup and two UEFA supercups. He won the gold medal with the Spanish Team in the Olimpics 1992 and he participated in the World Cup in 1994 and the Eurocup in 2000.

As a trainer, he was considered one of the best trainers in history, he directed FC Barcelonafrom 2008 to 2012, period when he won 14 titles. He won consecutively the six titles a team can win in an unic year in 2009 known in spanish as "sextete o sextuplete", he was the first trainer to complete this incredible achievement. He was nomined as best trainer in years 2009 and 2011 by the IFFHS. In 2010 he was galardoned with the Gold medal of the Real Order of Deportive Merit, the biggest and most important single distintion you can get in Spain.

From 2013 to 2016 he trained FC Bayern Múnich and won 7 titles in that period: one Europe Supercup, one Clup World Cup, three Bundescup and two German Cups.

From 2016 to today he is training Manchester City and he has won 11 titles in national level. Also, he became the first trainer winning the four titles in England in an unique year in the season of 2018-2019 and he made history making his club clasifying to the Champions League for the first time.

Eduardo Mendoza

Date of birth: 11 January 1943

He is a spanish writter with a narrative style simple and direct without forgetting to use cultisms, arcaisms like the popular language in his purest expression. He is used to write lonely characters that look wierdly to the society while they fight to survive being out of t.

His literary art, that starts with the publication of La verdad sobre el caso Savolta is generally ambiented in his natal city Barcelona, combined with the description of the city in times before spanish civil war and nowadays. Llazzer Moix talks about -bifront reality- when talking about Mendoza's narrative and he stablishes a distintion between his serious or mayor novels, and his amusements and minor novels. But the division between serius and humoristic novels that would correspond with the mayor and minor pointed, dont realize the narrative and estilistic richness of his parodic novels and the humorism present in his serious novels. That's why recent studios mark the seriuosness, criticism and trascendence in his horistic novels and the transversal humorism in his serious or mayor novels, as a result of the parodic effect carasteristic in the posmodern narrative.

Even thought his primal literary genre is the novel, Mendoza has written in his professional trayectory a lot of trials like Baroja, la contradicción and more recently stories like Tres vidas de santos. He recieved in 2010 the Planet award for his novel Riña de gatos and in 2016 the Cervantes award.
